## French 75

1. Chill coupe glass
2. Combine in shaker:
	- 1½ oz Gin
	- ¾ oz Lemon Juice
	- ½ oz Simple Syrup
3. Shake and pour in chilled coupe glass
	- 2 oz Dry champagne
4. Curl lemon peel round finger to create twist at least 6 inches long


__Alternative__

	- 1 oz Gin
	- 1 oz fresh lemon juice
	- ½ oz Simple Syrup
	- 2 oz chilled Champagne or sparkling wine
