## Atty

1. Add the following into a mixing glass (with ice):
	- 2 oz Dry Gin
	- ⅔ oz Dry Vermouth
	- 1/24 oz Crème de violette liqueur
	- 1/24 oz La Fée La Fee Parisienne Absinthe
2. Stir and strain into chilled couple
3. Garnish with lemon zest twist
