## The Queen's Gambit

Inspired by the TV series & book, the Queen's Gambit is a take on the Gibson that includes a Parisian influence.

1. Chill coupe glass
2. Combine in shaker:
	- 1½ oz Gin
	- ½ oz Simple Syrup
	- ¼ oz Lime Juice
	- ¼ oz Green Chartreuse
        - ⅛ oz St Germain
3. Shake and pour in chilled coupe glass
