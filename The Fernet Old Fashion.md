# The Fernet Old Fashioned

1. Add the following to a shaker:
	- Two "coins" of fresh ginger
	- A few drops of bitters
2. Lightly muddle
3. Add ice
4. Add the following to the shaker:
	- ½ oz maple syrup
	- ¾ oz Fernet Branca
	- 1½ oz Bourbon
5. Pour over a bourbon glass with ice, garnish with an orange peel
